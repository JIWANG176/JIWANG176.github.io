Ensemble methods are considered the state-of-the art solution for many machine learning challenges. Such methods improve the predictive performance of a single model by training multiple models and combining their predictions. This paper introduce the concept of ensemble learning, reviews traditional, novel and state-ofthe-art ensemble methods and discusses current challenges and trends in the field.
